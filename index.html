PROJECT & DEVELOPMENT

Built for a more
resource-conscious campus.

MCC Waste Exchange was conceived as a practical digital platform to encourage responsible material circulation within Madras Christian College. By making reuse and recycling easier to coordinate, the project aims to reduce avoidable waste and extend the useful life of everyday items.
